Milk Cream Cake

Description

Milk Cream Cake is an excellent cake for milk lovers. It features two types of milk: condensed milk and sour cream and optionally can be topped with whipped cream.

Cooking time: ~2hr 30min

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 5
  • Sugar: 170g(~1 glass)
  • Flour: ~1 glass
  • Baking powder: ~1 teaspoon
  • Vanilla sugar: ~1 teaspoon
  • Lemons: 2
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Berries(cherries or cranberries recommended): 150g
  • Condensed milk: 2/3 can
  • Sour cream: 600g

Steps

Sponge

  1. Take eggs and lemons out of the fridge in advance, so that they get warm.
  2. Wash lemons and grate the zest
  3. Cut zested lemons in half and extract the juice. Use sieve to filter out the seeds
  4. Turn on the oven and set the tempreature to 120C, so that it is prepared for baking
  5. Whip eggs and sugar with a mixer during no less than 12 minutes
  6. Add lemon zest
  7. Add the flour with baking powder to the whipped eggs. Do it gradually in three steps. Use the mixer at minimum speed.
  8. Add 2 tablespoons of lemon juice and 1 tablespoon of oil.
  9. Bake in the oven with the tempreature 170-180C. After ~10 minutes start checking it by piercing it with a match or a toothpick. As soon as the toothpick goes out clean, the sponge is ready.
  10. Take the sponge out of the oven and let it cool down for while. Then cover it with a plastic foil and put in the fridge.

Cream

  1. Put sour cream in a bowl and whip it with a mixer for a while
  2. Gradually add condensed milk to the sour cream, keeping the mixer on
  3. Add oil, lemon juice and oil
  4. Mix the cream for 2-3 minutes
  5. Put the cream in the fridge for several hours

Assembling the Cake

  1. Cut the sponge in two equal circular layers
  2. Take the bottom part and spread some cream on it
  3. Put some berries on top of it
  4. Cover the berries with another layer of cream and put the second sponge cirle on top
  5. Cover the entire circle of the cake with cream
  6. Decorate it with some berries on top
  7. Enjoy the cake!
