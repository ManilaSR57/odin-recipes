Milk Cream Cake

Description

Cooking time: ~1hr 30min

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 5
  • Sugar: 170g(~1 glass)
  • Flour: ~1 glass
  • Baking powder: ~1 teaspoon
  • Vanilla sugar: ~1 teaspoon
  • Lemons: 2
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Berries(cherries or cranberries recommended): 150g

Steps

Sponge

  • Take eggs and lemons out of the fridge in advance, so that they get warm.
  • Wash lemons and grate the zest
  • Cut zested lemons in half and extract the juice. Use sieve to filter out the seeds
  • Turn on the oven and set the tempreature to 120C, so that it is prepared for baking
  • Whip eggs and sugar with a mixer during no less than 12 minutes
  • Add lemon zest
  • Add the flour with baking powder to the whipped eggs. Do it gradually in three steps. Use the mixer at minimum speed.
  • Add 2 tablespoons of lemon juice and 1 tablespoon of oil.
  • Bake in the oven with the tempreature 170-180C. After ~10 minutes start checking it by piercing it with a match or a toothpick. As soon as the toothpick goes out clean, the sponge is ready.
  • Take the sponge out of the oven and let it cool down for while. Then cover it with a plastic foil and put in the fridge.
