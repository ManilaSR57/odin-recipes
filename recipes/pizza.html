Pizza Classic

Description

Delicious Classic Pizza is a perfect option for a dinner with friends and can be prepared with ingredients available at any local shop. Smoked pepperoni with juicy mushrooms and tomatoes covered with cheddar will certainly not leave your guest indifferent.

Cooking time: ~1hr 30min

Ingredients for 2-3 pizzas

  • Milk: 2 glasses
  • Salt: 2 tablespoons
  • Butter: 2 teaspoons
  • Yeast: 2 teaspoons
  • Egg: 1
  • Flour: until dough stops being sticky
  • Smoked sausage: 1 bar (~600g)
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese: ~250g
  • Tomatoes: ~400g
  • Mushrooms

Steps

Preparing Dough

  1. Mix the salt and butter in a one glass of milk and heat it up in a microwave oven until butter melts (~1min 30s).
  2. Mix the heated milk with one glass of cold milk.
  3. Add egg and yeast
  4. Add flour and knead the dough until it stops being sticky to your fingers
  5. Cover the dough with plastic foil and leave for 1-2 hours

Assembling Pizza

  1. Roll out a piece of dough size roughly of your fist into a thin layer. Shape is optional, but if you want a round pizza, you can put a pot lid (or anything of round shape you have) on top of the dough and cut out a circle.
  2. Put the dough on a tray with baking paper. You should lube the paper with some oil in advance.
  3. Spread around 2-3 tablespoons of ketchup on the dough.
  4. Cut some sausage into thin circles and put on the pizza
  5. Cut tomatoes and mushrooms into thin slices of any shape and put on the pizza
  6. Sprinkle some grated cheese on top
  7. Bake pizza with tempreature 170-180C
  8. Time the pizza should be baked dependes on your oven. General advice is to wait for 15 minutes and the carefully look after it. Then, check it regularly and take it out as soon as its underneath gets dark. Don't wait too long though, as you may burn it!
  9. Enjoy your pizza!
